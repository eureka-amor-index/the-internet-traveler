About

A small handmade internet shrine inspired by old websites, digital ghosts, unstable connections, pixel dreams, and the strange emotional architecture of the web.

✦ best viewed while emotionally attached to old internet aesthetics ✦

Who is the Internet Traveler?

The Internet Traveler is a fictional-digital persona wandering through old hyperlinks, forgotten pages, archives, guestbooks, blinking graphics, and abandoned corners of the internet.

This project was created by Eureka Amor as a nostalgic love letter to the early web... when websites felt personal, weird, emotional, and alive.

Somewhere between a diary, an art project, a digital shrine, and an internet memory system.
